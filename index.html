World of Nexus

A City Alive
With Power

Welcome to Nexus — a vast cyberpunk cityscape where five factions are locked in a relentless struggle for dominance. Every card you play, every strategy you deploy, rewrites the destiny of this world.

As a player, you don't just witness the war for Nexus — you are its architect. Build your deck, pick your syndicate, and make every move count.

How It Works

Built for Strategists

01

Build Your Deck

Construct a minimum 60-card deck around your chosen Leader. Your Leader locks in your faction — only cards from that faction plus neutral Free Agents are legal. Max 3 copies of any single card.

02

Tech Level System

Each turn you gain Tech Points equal to your Tech Level — your currency for playing cards. Attach Programs to your Leader to raise your Tech Level and unlock bigger plays. Unspent points carry over, rewarding patience.

03

Combat & Hacks

Deploy Units to your Server and attack your opponent's Units or Leader directly. Hack cards can be played at instant speed — even on your opponent's turn — and resolve in last-in-first-out order, creating deep tactical exchanges.
