How It Works

Built for Strategists

01

Build Your Deck

Construct a minimum 60-card deck around your chosen Leader. Your Leader's faction determines which cards are legal — only cards from their faction plus neutral Free Agents are allowed. Max 3 copies of any card.

02

Spend Tech Points

Each turn you gain Tech Points equal to your Tech Level. Spend them to deploy Units, play Hacks, attach Programs, or add Upgrades. Unspent points carry over — save up for your biggest plays.

03

Destroy the Leader

Win by reducing your opponent's Leader HP to zero through combat, or force them to draw from an empty deck. Units with Taunt must be attacked first — clear the board before going for the Leader directly.

The Cards

Five Types. One Battlefield.

L

Leader

Your champion. Starts in play, defines your faction, and sets your starting Tech Level. If their HP hits zero — you lose.

U

Unit

Fighters deployed to your Server. They attack with their ATK stat and survive until their HP hits zero. Damage sticks between turns.

H

Hack

Instant-speed cards playable at any time — even on your opponent's turn. They stack and resolve last-in-first-out via the Priority system.

P

Program

Attach to your Leader to raise your Tech Level and gain more resources each turn. Max 2 Programs at once — a third replaces one.

G

Upgrade

Attach to a Unit to enhance its stats or grant new abilities. Each Unit holds 1 Upgrade at a time unless its card text says otherwise.
