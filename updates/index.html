Dev Log

Development Updates

Follow the evolution of Neon Nexus from prototype to full release. Every build, every revision, every decision — documented here.

March 4, 2026 Latest
Artwork

Replacing Placeholder Art with Original Pieces

The team is actively working on replacing all placeholder AI-generated artwork with original pieces created by the founder and collaborating artists. This is a key step in the development of Neon Nexus TCG, and updates will be shared as new art is revealed.

October 4, 2024
Rules

Rules Page Live — Version 1.0

The official Neon Nexus rulebook is now available on the site. Version 1.0 covers all core gameplay, card types, keywords, and win conditions. Bookmark it and bring it to your next game session.

March 13, 2024
Site Cards

New Domain + Full Card Catalog Refresh

Transferred to the new permanent domain. Every card in the catalog has been updated with the latest artwork and visual layout, including a round of typo corrections across all card text.

March 1, 2024
Cards

Leader & Program Card Redesign

Leader and Program cards have been redesigned to make them immediately distinguishable from Unit cards at a glance. Each card now displays a faction icon to make identification faster during play — no more squinting at color alone.

December 8, 2023
Physical Print

Print Test — Leader & Program Circuit Redesign

After receiving physical test prints of the cards, the circuit attachment graphics on Leader cards and Programs were too faint to read clearly. These have been redesigned to be more visible at card size. The catalog will be updated to reflect the new versions.

November 2, 2023
Tabletop Sim

All Cards Imported to Tabletop Simulator

All Neon Nexus cards have been imported into Tabletop Simulator so you can play online right now. Join the Discord and request access — we'll get you set up to play test the current build.

November 1, 2023
Community

Discord Server Launched

The official Neon Nexus Discord is open. Come talk strategy, ask rules questions, get early access to Tabletop Simulator, and follow development in real time. Link is in the nav above.
